Final Program Assignment: Monster Defender

The current project is a short arcade style video game mockup.
The concept of this game is that you are a Mage Character trying to
fight off the evil monsters trying to take over the world. As the Mage, you will control him with keys W, A, S & D to navigagte.
To attack the monsters you will use the mouse to click and shoot the Mage's beam. When the Mage's beam collides with the Monsters, then the Hero
kills it and gains a point. The theme of this game reminds me of various PC MMO's given that a Mage is involved. So I wanted to do a twist on an arcade game that looks like an arcade/pixel
game but give a little feel of MMO's based on the character choice.
